You Are Under Attack!

Now is a great time to be a hacker. IoT devices are becoming more popular while exposing massive security holes in home networks. Tools like Kali Linux and Metasploit now make hacking as easy as pushing a button. 0 Days can be bought on the dark web. Your finances, web cams, photos, passwords, etc. are there for the taking unless you are proactive about network security.

HANK Makes Network Security Easy

HANK works by listening for signs of a network intrusion. When an intrusion is detected HANK turns off power to your router, effectively shutting out the intrudor. Even better, HANK requires zero configuration. You just plug it in and walk away.
